Warp, MileStone 2, Marcus McGuire 
Controls: 
shoot: space
jump: 'w', or 's'
time warp(switch between present and future): right mouse click.
Current game mechanics:
-switching between past and present in order to navigate platforms and solve puzzles
-minor enemies 
-hazards(lava)
-switches(which can be interacted with via the laser
-Destructable Teal objects
-a health item which increases the players maximum health, hidden in one of the levels
-a blue vile as the objective for this level.
-The Goal of this game is to achieve three vials and defeat the boss to escape the lab.
